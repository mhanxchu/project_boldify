# Concept: Personal Communication Coach

## Problem Statement

Many people feel anxious during interviews and important conversations because they struggle to express themselves clearly and confidently.

## Target Audience

Our focus is on **job seekers** and **early-career professionals** who need to make a strong impression but often feel unsure or overwhelmed.

## Solution

We're building a **personal communication coach** that:

- Understands their emotional state
- Simulates real-life situations
- Guides them with tailored feedback

## Vision

A supportive AI-powered platform that helps individuals build confidence and improve their communication skills through personalized practice and feedback, specifically designed for high-stakes situations like job interviews and professional conversations.
